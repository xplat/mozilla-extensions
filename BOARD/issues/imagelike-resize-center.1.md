During smooth (drag or animated) resizes, the `ResizeObserver` in `viewer-media-imagelike.js` applies incremental scroll deltas to keep the visible center stable, but the fractional pixels are discarded at each step — so the center drifts noticeably when many small resize events fire in quick succession.  The resize logic is also local to the imagelike module rather than coordinated through `viewer-transform.js`, which may or may not be the right home for it.
